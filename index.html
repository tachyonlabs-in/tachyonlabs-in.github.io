Flagship Product

Sakhi: Advancing Digital Health Literacy

Sakhi is our innovative platform advancing digital health literacy among mothers in rural India. Through a WhatsApp-based multimodal chatbot and integration with community health workers, we provide personalized health information and support.

  • MIT and UNICEF-incubated tech venture improving women's health literacy
  • WhatsApp-based multimodal chatbot with human-in-the-loop design
  • Launched maternal health pilot with 140+ families in Jalgaon, India
  • Menstrual health pilot with 200+ families in Dhaka, Bangladesh
